# Privacy Policy - Indalo Padel

**Effective date:** April 10, 2026  
**Last updated:** April 10, 2026

Indalo Padel (the "App") is a mobile application for padel court bookings and match organization.

This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we process, why we process it, who we share it with, and your rights.

## 1. Data controller

- **Controller:** Indalo Padel
- **Privacy contact email:** `sergio.hlara84@gmail.com`

## 2. Personal data we collect

1. **Account data**
- Name
- Email
- Password (stored as hash, never in plain text)

2. **Player profile data**
- Display name
- Skill level and preferences
- Bio and avatar (if provided)

3. **Core app usage data**
- Bookings (date, time, court, venue, status, notes)
- Matches (creation, join/leave actions, status, description)
- Favorites and player ratings/comments

4. **Minimal technical data**
- Server technical logs (requests and errors)
- Session token stored securely on device

## 3. Why we process your data

We process personal data to:

1. Create and manage user accounts.
2. Enable bookings and match workflows.
3. Provide profile and social features.
4. Maintain security and prevent abuse.
5. Comply with legal obligations.

## 4. Legal bases (GDPR)

1. Performance of a contract (app service delivery).
2. Legitimate interest (security and service integrity).
3. Legal obligations.
4. Consent, where required for specific features.

## 5. Data sharing

We do not sell personal data.

We may share data with service providers required to operate the App:

1. **Infrastructure and database**
- Render (backend hosting)
- Supabase/PostgreSQL (data storage)

2. **Optional calendar integration**
- Google Calendar API (when calendar sync is enabled)

## 6. International transfers

Some providers may process data outside the EEA.  
Where applicable, lawful transfer safeguards are applied (for example, Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms).

## 7. Data retention

We keep personal data for as long as:

1. Your account remains active.
2. Data is needed to provide the service.
3. Legal obligations require retention.

When no longer needed, data is securely deleted or anonymized.

## 8. Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational safeguards, including:

1. Password hashing.
2. Token-based authentication.
3. Server-side database access controls.
4. Basic error and security monitoring.

No system is fully risk-free, but we continuously improve controls.

## 9. Your rights

Subject to applicable law, you may request:

1. Access.
2. Rectification.
3. Erasure.
4. Objection.
5. Restriction of processing.
6. Data portability.

To exercise rights, contact: `sergio.hlara84@gmail.com`

You can also file a complaint with your competent supervisory authority.

## 10. Children

The App is not specifically directed to children under 14.  
If we detect unauthorized processing of children's data, we will take steps to remove it.
